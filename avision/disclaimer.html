The purpose of this web site is to explore typography and text layout through time, as an end-of-course project for the "Information Modeling and Web technologies" course of the Master Degree in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge of the University of Bologna, under prof. Fabio Vitali.

The documents contained in this web site have been selected for their length and complexity among the best picks of 2018 of www.longreads.com. Their publication here is not intended to be an alternative or replace their original locations:
