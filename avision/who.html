We are students of the master's programme 'Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge', offered by the University of Bologna. Our group comprises of 3 members presented below:

Antonatou Ioanna

She was born in Athens, Greece in 1996. She completed her bachelor studies in English Language and Literature in the University of Athens in 2018 and is currently attending the Master’s programme “Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge”, offered by the University of Bologna, Italy. Her academic interests include sociolinguistics, discourse in social media, digital text and knowledge organization.

Gollini Mariachiara

She was born in Italy in 1995. She completed a bachelor in Lettere moderne at the university of Bologna and is currently attending the Master's programme “Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge”. Her academic interests include text analysis and knowledge representation.

Lučić Katarina

She was born in Nikšić, Montenegro in 1994. After obtaining a Bachelor's degree in Italian studies at the University of Belgrade, she decided to broaden her knowledge while using her academic background, so she's currently attending a Master's degree course in Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge at the University of Bologna. Her academic interests, other than those offered by the course curriculum, also include the fields such as music informatics and music encoding
