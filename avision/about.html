About the project

For the purposes of the course 'Information Modeling and Web Technologies', offered by the master degree 'Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge' of the University of Bologna, we created a web magazine, the main purpose of which is to demonstrate the course of typography through time. We have created six themes representative of different historical periods, which render the text compliant to the typographical rules of that time.

Going back -and forth- in time was no easy venture, but TARDIS got us through the hardships; the ride was educative, but also fun! :)
